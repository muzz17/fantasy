Fantasy Football Player Rankings!

This site ranks fantasy football players according to both the player's average weekly score and the player's weekly consistency.

The variance ranking ranks players according to their standard deviation of weekly scores. Less variance means a more consistent player week to week.

The scoring average ranking ranks players according to their average weekly score. A higher average score means a more productive player.

The master ranking combines both the variance ranking and the average score ranking to find the most balanced players. The lower the number, the better the player. These players are the best combination of both consistency and production. On all charts, the best player for that particular chart is located at the top of the y-axis.
